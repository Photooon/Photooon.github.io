About Me

I am a final-year master student in Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Yangdong Deng. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Wuhan University in June 2022.

Efficiency lies at the heart of computer science. My current focus is on efficient machine learning, where I am deeply committed to exploring innovative approaches that bridge algorithmic advancements and system-level optimizations. I firmly believe that significant leaps in efficiency can only be achieved through a harmonious integration of these two perspectives.

Outside of research, I enjoy running, swimming and books on history and sociology.
